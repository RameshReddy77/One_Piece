Summit War

The Summit War Saga marks one of the most emotional and intense periods in One Piece, covering episodes 385 to 516. It begins with the Sabaody Archipelago Arc, where the Straw Hats finally reunite after Fish-Man Island planning but immediately clash with the Celestial Dragons, Pacifistas, and Admiral Kizaru. Their adventure takes a tragic turn when Bartholomew Kuma uses his power to separate the crew, sending each member flying to a different location around the world. This marks the first time Luffy is completely helpless as his entire crew vanishes before his eyes.

Luffy lands in the all-female island Amazon Lily, where he meets Boa Hancock. Despite her hatred for men, Hancock falls deeply in love with Luffy and eventually helps him sneak into Impel Down to rescue his brother, Portgas D. Ace, who is scheduled for execution at Marineford. Inside Impel Down (Episodes 422–425, 430–458), Luffy battles through each level of the deadly underwater prison. He re-encounters Buggy, Mr. 3, Bon Clay, Crocodile, Ivankov, and Jinbe, forming an unlikely alliance as he fights Warden Magellan. Although Luffy fails to find Ace in time, he pushes himself to near death to break out and reach Marineford.

The Marineford War Arc (Episodes 457–489) stands as the peak of the saga. The Whitebeard Pirates clash with the entire Marine Headquarters to save Ace. Luffy arrives mid-war, desperately fighting to reach his brother. Despite their combined efforts, Ace is tragically killed by Admiral Akainu while protecting Luffy. Whitebeard also dies after declaring that the One Piece is real, shaking the world. This war forever alters Luffy, physically and emotionally.

The saga concludes with the Post-War Arc (Episodes 490–516), where Luffy mourns Ace deeply at Amazon Lily. Jinbe helps him face his pain, reminding him that he still has something left—his crew. Luffy decides they need more strength before returning to the New World, sending a secret message for the Straw Hats to train for 2 years instead of reuniting immediately. Each member begins their time-skip training, preparing for the world that awaits beyond the Grand Line.

This saga reshapes the story, changes the balance of power, reveals major world secrets, and sets Luffy on a new path of growth. It stands as one of the most emotional, legendary arcs in anime history.

  • Sabaody Archipelago Arc: Episodes 385–405
  • Amazon Lily Arc: Episodes 408–421
  • Impel Down Arc: Episodes 422–458
  • Little East Blue (Filler): Episodes 426–429
  • Marineford Arc: Episodes 459–489
  • Post–War Arc: Episodes 490–516
