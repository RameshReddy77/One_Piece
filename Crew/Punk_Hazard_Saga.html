Punk Hazard

The Punk Hazard Saga marks the beginning of the New World-era adventures of the Straw Hat Pirates, filled with strange environments, dangerous experiments, and major revelations that affect the entire story going forward. The saga begins when the Straw Hats receive a mysterious distress call from a man begging for help on an island that supposedly shouldn’t exist. Despite warnings, Luffy decides to investigate, leading the crew to the hazardous Punk Hazard Island. The island is split into two extremes: one half burning with eternal flames and the other frozen in ice, the result of the legendary Aokiji vs. Akainu battle that reshaped the world.

Upon landing, the crew encounters bizarre creatures, giant dragons, and scorched landscapes. Soon, they discover that Punk Hazard is controlled by the mad scientist Caesar Clown, a former scientist of the World Government who now conducts illegal and deadly experiments. Caesar is producing SAD, a chemical used to create artificial Devil Fruits called SMILEs, which are later used by Kaido’s Beast Pirates. This is the first major hint of the Yonko's deeper operations in the New World.

The Straw Hats ally with Trafalgar D. Water Law, now a Shichibukai, who proposes a shocking alliance: take down Kaido. Law becomes a major central character, and this saga is the foundation for the future Dressrosa and Wano arcs. Meanwhile, Caesar kidnaps children and experiments on them, enlarging them and making them addicted to drugs. The Straw Hats, especially Nami and Chopper, are furious and vow to rescue them.

Sanji, Franky, Nami, and Chopper are captured temporarily and discover Caesar’s terrifying “Shinokuni” gas weapon, capable of turning people into stone-like corpses. At the same time, Luffy, Zoro, Robin, and Usopp join forces with the samurai Kin’emon, who is searching for his son Momonosuke. This father-son storyline becomes crucial for the Wano Country Saga later.

The battle intensifies when Caesar unleashes his pet slime monster and his elite guards, but the Straw Hats overwhelm them. Luffy defeats Caesar in a brutal fight, ending his cruelty and drug production. The destruction of SAD shakes the entire underworld and angers Doflamingo, who moves to intercept the Straw Hats.

The saga ends with the Straw Hats escaping the island with Law, Kin’emon, Momonosuke, and the rescued children. Doflamingo receives orders from a higher power (later revealed to be Kaido) to punish Law and Luffy. This final moment sets the stage for the massive Dressrosa Saga.

  • Punk Hazard Arc: Episodes 579–625
  • Caesar Retrieval Arc (Filler): Episodes 626–628
