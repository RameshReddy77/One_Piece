Skypiea

The Sky Island Saga begins when the crew departs Alabasta and encounters a pirate ship falling from the sky, leading them to investigate the mysterious Knock-Up Stream. After a tense journey upward, they arrive in the White Sea and learn of a higher realm—the Sky Island known as Skypiea. The early part of the saga features comedic misunderstandings, heavenly landscapes, and the introduction of the waver, a sky vehicle ridden by Nami. The crew is then accused of illegal entry by the Skypiean authorities, discovering that the land is under the tyrannical rule of “God” Eneru, whose lightning powers make him nearly invincible.

Exploration reveals the ancient conflict between Skypieans and the Shandians, who fight over their ancestral homeland. The crew visits Upper Yard, discovering it is a piece of the legendary Jaya that was blasted upward centuries ago. Robin begins uncovering Poneglyph-related mysteries, adding deeper lore to the world. Meanwhile, Eneru’s plan for “Survival Game” reduces the island’s population, gradually setting up the final showdown. Luffy, Zoro, Robin, Wyper, and others face divine soldiers and ordeal-based trials.

The climax centers on Luffy’s battle against Eneru, where his rubber body makes him immune to lightning—an iconic “image moment” showing Luffy standing firm against Eneru’s divine strikes. The Golden Bell, key to the Shandians’ history, becomes the emotional symbol of the saga. Luffy’s final punch rings the bell, sending a signal back down to Jaya and fulfilling the dream of the explorer Mont Blanc Cricket. The saga ends with a celebratory feast, a restored island, and a deeper sense of wonder about the world.
