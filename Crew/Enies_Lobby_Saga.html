The Water 7 & Enies Lobby Saga is one of the most emotional and action-packed sagas in One Piece, covering episodes 207 to 325. The saga begins when the Straw Hat Pirates arrive at Water 7 in search of a shipwright to fix the Going Merry. They encounter the Galley-La Company and learn about the city's unique water-based lifestyle. Soon, a shocking revelation occurs—Mary is declared beyond repair, shattering the crew emotionally and causing a major conflict between Luffy and Usopp. Meanwhile, mysterious agents known as CP9 infiltrate the story, revealing that Robin has agreed to abandon the crew to protect them from the World Government. This leads to one of the most iconic rescue missions in anime history.

The saga escalates at Enies Lobby, a massive judicial island where Robin is imprisoned. Here, Luffy and the Straw Hats declare war on the World Government by burning the World Government flag, symbolizing their refusal to abandon their friend. Robin's emotional plea—“I want to live!”—becomes one of the most unforgettable moments in the series. The crew battles CP9, each member showcasing powerful new abilities, such as Luffy’s Gear Second and Gear Third. After defeating Rob Lucci in a legendary fight, they escape Enies Lobby thanks to the unexpected return of the Going Merry, who appears one last time to save them before receiving a proper farewell.

The saga ends with the arrival of the Marines, Garp’s introduction, and the crew’s reunion with a new ship—the Thousand Sunny, built by their newest crewmate Franky. Usopp returns after apologizing, and the crew grows stronger both emotionally and physically. Overall, this saga highlights themes of loyalty, sacrifice, friendship, and the harsh reality of the sea, making it one of the most memorable and beloved sagas in One Piece history.

  • Long Ring Long Land Arc: Episodes 207–219
  • Ocean's Dream Arc (Filler): Episodes 220–224
  • Foxy's Return Arc (Filler): Episodes 225–228
  • Water 7 Arc: Episodes 229–263
  • Enies Lobby Arc: Episodes 264–312
  • Post-Enies Lobby Arc: Episodes 313–325
