Dressrosa

The Dressrosa Saga marks one of the most important turning points in One Piece, as it establishes the Straw Hat Pirates as a major force in the New World and directly sets the stage for the fall of the Four Emperors. It begins with the Punk Hazard Arc, where the crew responds to a distress call and ends up on a burning-and-freezing island sliced in half by Aokiji and Akainu during their historic duel. Here, the Straw Hats encounter Caesar Clown, a sadistic scientist working under Donquixote Doflamingo. They rescue kidnapped children being experimented on and form the legendary alliance with Trafalgar Law, whose goal is to take down Kaido. The crew also reunites unexpectedly with Smoker and Tashigi and battles the terrifying gas weapon Shinokuni. Punk Hazard ends with Law capturing Caesar and officially declaring war on Doflamingo.

The saga then moves to the heart of the conflict: Dressrosa, a vibrant island ruled by the Shichibukai Doflamingo, who pretends to be a beloved king while secretly controlling the kingdom with cruelty and deception. Dressrosa is home to living toys, gladiator arenas, dwarves, and powerful assassins in Doflamingo’s Donquixote Family. Luffy enters the Corrida Colosseum under the name “Lucy” to win Ace’s Mera Mera no Mi, clashing with gladiators like Cavendish, Bartolomeo, Chinjao, and the legendary Kyros. Meanwhile, Law executes his plan to bring Doflamingo down, revealing the dark past of the Donquixote Family and the tragedy of Dressrosa’s former king, Riku.

Over the course of the arc, many shocking truths are revealed: toys are actually transformed humans, Doflamingo manipulates the entire country using the String-String Fruit, and the ancient warrior Kyros has been erased from history. The Straw Hats split into multiple teams, fighting elite officers such as Trebol, Pica, Diamante, and Sugar. Usopp becomes "God Usopp" after sniping Sugar from an impossible distance. Zoro cuts Pica’s gigantic stone body. Sabo returns as the second-in-command of the Revolutionary Army and eats Ace’s Mera Mera no Mi, becoming Flame Emperor Sabo.

The climax arrives with Luffy activating Gear Fourth (Boundman) for the first time, engaging in an explosive battle with Doflamingo across the skies of Dressrosa. After a grueling fight, Luffy’s final King Kong Gun ends Doflamingo’s reign. The world reacts with shock as a Yonko-targeted alliance and the Straw Hats topple one of the most notorious Shichibukai. This saga ends with the formation of the Straw Hat Grand Fleet, consisting of seven crews who pledge their loyalty to Luffy after being inspired by his actions. Dressrosa’s liberation changes the balance of power across the world, intensifies the tension with Kaido, and sets the Straw Hats on a direct collision course with the New World’s strongest forces.

  • Punk Hazard Arc: Episodes 579–625
  • Caesar Retrieval Arc (Filler): Episodes 626–628
  • Dressrosa Arc: Episodes 629–746
